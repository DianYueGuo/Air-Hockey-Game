# C1

What changed
- Added an audio manager that loads default sound assets with pygame.mixer.
- Hooked puck-wall and puck-mallet collisions to play sounds.
- Added goal sound playback on scoring and generated placeholder WAV assets.

Manual test steps
- Run `python -m air_hockey.main`.
- Click Play and move a mallet to hit the puck; listen for hit sounds.
- Score a goal and confirm the goal sound plays.

Known issues
- Sound levels are placeholder tones.
